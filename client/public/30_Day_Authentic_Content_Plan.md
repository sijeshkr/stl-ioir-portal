**Full Post Copy:**
```
Y90 radioembolization is one of the most fascinating procedures in Interventional Oncology.

Here's how it works (in simple terms):

We inject millions of tiny radioactive beads (smaller than a grain of sand) directly into the blood vessels feeding a liver tumor.

The beads get stuck in the tumor's blood supply and deliver targeted radiation from the inside.

The tumor gets maximum radiation. The healthy liver gets minimal exposure.

I've performed over 400 of these procedures. Every time, I'm amazed by the precision.

Not every liver cancer patient is a candidate. But for those who are, it can extend life and improve quality of life significantly.
```
