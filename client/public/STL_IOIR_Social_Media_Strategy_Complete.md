## Executive Summary

STL IOIR is launching as the **first independent, office-based Interventional Oncology and Interventional Radiology clinic in St. Louis**. This represents a unique positioning opportunity in a market dominated by hospital-based care.

### Strategic Approach: **"Authority First, Referrals Primary"**

Unlike typical medical practices that focus on direct patient acquisition, STL IOIR's success depends primarily on **physician referrals** (Medical Oncologists, Surgical Oncologists, GI specialists, Primary Care). Therefore, our social media strategy inverts the traditional approach.

**Priority Hierarchy:**
1. **LinkedIn** (60% effort) - Build authority with referring physicians
2. **Facebook** (30% effort) - Educate patients and families who research options
3. **Instagram** (10% effort) - Secondary patient awareness channel

### Three-Phase Strategy

**Phase 1: PRE-LAUNCH (Feb 18 - March 7)** - Awareness & Anticipation  
Build credibility, announce opening, educate market on IR/IO

**Phase 2: LAUNCH WEEK (March 8-14)** - Grand Opening Momentum  
Coordinated multi-platform launch, maximize visibility, drive appointments

**Phase 3: POST-LAUNCH (March 15 - May 31)** - Education & Conversion  
Procedure deep-dives, patient testimonials, case studies, referral cultivation

#### Post 3: What is Interventional Oncology?
**Copy:**
"❓ What is Interventional Oncology?

If you or a loved one has been diagnosed with cancer, you've likely heard about surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation. But there's another powerful option many people don't know about: **Interventional Oncology (IO)**.

**Here's how it works:**

Instead of open surgery, IO uses tiny, pinhole-sized incisions (less than 3mm) to deliver highly targeted cancer treatments directly to tumors — guided by advanced imaging.

🎯 **Y90 Therapy** — Delivers radiation beads directly into liver tumors

✅ No large incisions or "cutting"  
✅ Less pain and faster recovery

**Who can benefit?**  
Patients with liver, kidney, bone, or thyroid cancers — especially those who aren't candidates for surgery or want a less invasive option.

#### Post 5: Y90 Therapy Spotlight
**Copy:**
"🎯 Liver Cancer Treatment: Y90 Radioembolization

If you or a loved one has liver cancer, you may have heard that surgery isn't an option. But that doesn't mean there's no hope.

**Y90 Therapy** is a cutting-edge treatment that delivers targeted radiation directly to liver tumors — without surgery, without external radiation, and with minimal side effects.

**How it works:**

Tiny radioactive beads (smaller than a grain of sand) are delivered through a catheter directly into the blood vessels feeding the tumor. The beads lodge in the tumor and emit radiation from the inside out, destroying cancer cells while sparing healthy liver tissue.

**Who can benefit?**  
• Patients with primary liver cancer (HCC)  
• Patients with liver metastases (cancer that spread to the liver)  
• Those who aren't candidates for surgery  
• Those who want a less invasive option

**The benefits:**  
✅ Outpatient procedure (go home the same day)  
✅ Minimal side effects compared to systemic chemo  
✅ Preserves healthy liver function
